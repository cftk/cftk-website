Important information regarding the Committee Member Application:

   • The application is two-fold. The first part consists of general questions that all applicants must answer. The second part is committee-specific (please only answer the questions that pertain the committees you selected as your top 3 preferences).
   • Type your answers into a document and then paste them into the application. This will help eliminate any technical errors that may arise (loss of network connectivity, accidentally navigating away from this page, etc).
   • Don't navigate away from this page while you are completing the application. If you would like to view the committee descriptions at the same time, it is recommended you do so using a separate tab/window. Be yourself and be sure to let your creative juices flow through your answers!

Please report any technical issues to technology@carolinaftk.org
